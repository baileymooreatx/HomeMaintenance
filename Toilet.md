# Toilet Maintenance  

The information comes from YouTube video
[Toilet Tank Trick Plumbers DON'T WANT YOU TO KNOW!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LhKGE44qSM0)
published by [Esther Yoder](https://www.youtube.com/@EstherYoderAmish).  

## Monthly  

Pour one or two cups of distilled white distilled vinegar into the overflow 
pipe in the toilet tank to keep mildew and mineral scale from growing inside 
the rim of the toilet.  

## Tablets  

Use homemade tablets in between vinegar treatments to keep the bowl clean and 
free from rust and mineral scale.  

### Materials  

* 1 cup Baking Soda  
* ½ cup Citric Acid  
* 1 tablespoon Dish Liquid  
* Optional: 10 to 15 drops of Essential Oil (for fragrance)
* Spray bottle willed with water  
* Ice cube tray or other mold for tablets  

### Instructions  

1. Mix the baking soda and citric acid together thoroughly, breaking up any 
   lumps.  
2. Mix in the dish liquid and essential oil.
3. Use the spray bottle to barely dampen the mixture while stirring in the 
   water. Use only enough water to make the mixture hold together like damp 
   sand.  
4. Press the mixture firmly into the ice tray or other mold.  
5. Let them sit for a day to dry and harden before removing them from the 
   mold. If they crumble, them needed slightly more water to hold together.  
6. Store the tablets in a jar with an airtight lid to keep them dry.

## Oxalic Acid  

The information comes from YouTube video
[$2 AMISH Powder REMOVES Any Bathroom Stain FOREVER No Chemicals. No Plumber. BURIED Since 1947](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LuxVUuO6LzM)
published by [Esther Yoder](https://www.youtube.com/@EstherYoderAmish).  

This gets rid of mineral and rust rings around the toilet bowl.  
Purchase oxalic acid at a hardware store or paint store. It is also called 
"wood bleach" or "rust remover crystals."  

### Instructions  

1. Turn the water valve off and flush the toilet to lower the water below the 
   stain ring.  
2. Mix two heaping spoons (about 1 oz) of oxalic acid into a cup of warm 
   water in a plastic container you don't value. Stir until the powder or 
   crystals are completely dissolved.   
3. Brush the solutions onto the stains with a cheap sponge brush.  
4. Let it stand for 20 to 30 minutes. **Do not scrub.**   
5. Turn the water valve back on and flush twice.

Badly scaled stains may require a second treatment.
For grout stains use a toothbrush.
Also works on rust stains around sink drains.
