# Scouring Paste  

This scouring paste comes from YouTube video
[Mix detergent with SALT 😱 You will not believe the incredible result](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8DFUbt5_rN0)
published by [Elias Yoder](https://www.youtube.com/@EliasYoderAmish).

## Materials  

* 1 cup Dish liquid
* 1 cup Warm water
* 2 tablespoons Table salt
* 1 tablespoons Baking soda

## Instructions  

1. Add the materials in the order listed.
2. Stir gently.
3. Store in a clean glass jar.

## Uses  

* Spread the paste with just enough water to wet it on the burnt on area.
* Let it stand for 10 to 15 minutes.
* Scrub with a soft brush or scouring pad.
* Very tough areas may require a second treatment.  

## Applications  

* Burnt pots and pans  
* Cooked on grease
* Kitchen sink  
* Stove top  
* Microwave interior  
* Stained plastic containers  
* Silverware and flatware  
* Bathroom fixtures: sink, tub, toilet
