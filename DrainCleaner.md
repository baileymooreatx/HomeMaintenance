# Drain Cleaner  

This comes from YouTube video 
[This $2 Mineral BEATS Every Drain Cleaner on the Market](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Jrb3zx4TlZc) 
published by [Elias Yoder](https://www.youtube.com/@EliasYoderAmish). 
It works for normal grease and hair clogs and reducing build up inside pipes.  

## Ingredients  

* ½ cup 
* ½ cup baking soda
* ¼ cup Pickling or coarse salt  

Clogged drains will also require  
* ½ cup of white distilled vinegar (used separately)  

## Instructions  

1. Mix the dry ingredients in a glass Mason jar.   
2. Seal the jar tightly.  
3. Label the jar.  
4. Store indefinitely under the sink or in a pantry.  

### Slow Drain (Maintenance)  

1. Pour ½ cup of powder into a dry drain.  
2. Pour 1 cup boiling water slowly over the powder to activate the alkaline 
   reaction.
3. Wait for at least 20 but up to 40 minutes, leaving the drain completely 
   undisturbed. Longer is better.  
4. Flush with a full kettle of boiling water to flush the dissolved crud 
   down the pipes.  
5. If the drain is still slow, repeat one more time.  

### Standing Water (Clogged) 

1. Scoop out as much standing water as you can.  
2. Pour ½ cup of powder into the dry drain.
3. Pout ¼ cup of distilled white vinegar over the powder.  
4. Wait for at 30 minutes, leaving the drain completely
   undisturbed.  
5. Flush with a full kettle of boiling water.  
6. If the drain is still clogged, repeat one more time.  
  
If the clog remains after two applications, call a plumber. 
The pipe is blocked, not clogged.  
Do not use commercial, sodium hydroxide drain cleaner.
